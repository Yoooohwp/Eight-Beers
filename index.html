About.

Eight Beers lahir dari eksplorasi rasa dan fermentasi lokal. Kami memadukan kesegaran pisang matang
dengan ragi premium untuk menghasilkan pengalaman minum yang tak terlupakan—ringan,
menyenangkan, dan penuh karakter tropis.

What We Stand For:

Local Goodness

At Eight Beers, we celebrate the warmth
of togetherness and local pride. Each
bottle is crafted with bananas grown by
nearby farmers — creating not just a
beverage, but a bond with the people
and land that nurture it.

Innovation in Brewing

Inspired by tradition, driven by curiosity. Our
brewing process blends classic fermentation
methods with bold new ideas — giving rise to
eight distinct Banana Beers
brewing process blends classic fermentation

Open Culture

We believe in transparency, collaboration,
and play. Our community welcomes makers,
drinkers, and thinkers to co-create, explore,
and toast to the unexpected. With every
bottle, you’re part of something open and alive.
